How it was made

Three independent methods were run over a corpus of 265 Middle Dutch rhymed manuscripts: TF‑IDF bigram/trigram similarity, a rhyme-word‑based General Impostors verification with leave-one-out validation against known multi-manuscript authors, and a contrastive character-level line encoder trained across the corpus with author identity as the training signal. The full pipeline and how to regenerate the data behind these tabs is documented in the README.
